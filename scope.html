Scope

The project intends to contribute to addressing the problems mentioned above by achieving two main objectives

  • designing multidisciplinary curricula for the creation of two professional profiles with European value: 1) European Crisis Emergency Manager (ECEM); 2) European Prevention Manager (EPM);
  • and to provide teachers with the competences and skills to design and implement a multidisciplinary modular curriculum offering a high degree of flexibility in the definition of the curriculum.

The main activities will be:

  1. identify existing profiles and curricula for the preparation of the emergency manager and analyse knowledge gaps and skills mismatches;
  2. design multidisciplinary curricula for the creation of the two professionals ECEM and EPM;
  3. create new degrees or new tracks in the curricula that best fit the ECEM and EPM profiles envisaged;
  4. designing modules to be integrated into the Health/Engineering/Management curricula to provide students in these degrees with the knowledge and skills needed to perform community service in a competent and appropriate manner, including as volunteers, and to enhance their active citizenship and civic engagement;
  5. implement new modules/pathways providing such knowledge and skills and organise work experience;
  6. to finalise the process for the academic certification of these competences.
